The Problem

Our members want to get stronger.
But they don't know how.

  • Generic fitness apps don't account for sparring fatigue
  • Members overtrain before class and underperform on the mat
  • No programming adjusts around BJJ, Muay Thai, or wrestling schedules
  • Injuries from lifting lead to missed mat time

The result?

Members either skip strength work entirely, follow cookie-cutter programs that wreck their recovery, or pay $150+/mo for a separate strength coach.

We're leaving money on the table and members undertrained.

02
The Solution

An app that programs strength training around martial arts.

Roots Gains auto-generates periodized lifting programs that reduce volume on hard sparring days, peak for competitions, and adjust in real-time based on recovery data.

🏋

Combat-Aware

Auto-reduces lifting on heavy sparring days. Never walk onto the mat pre-fatigued again.

🧠

Science-Backed

Daily Undulating Periodization (DUP) rotates strength, hypertrophy, and power for optimal gains without burnout.

⚡

Auto-Regulated

Whoop integration reads your recovery and adjusts today's workout intensity automatically.

03
Key Features

Everything a martial artist needs. Nothing they don't.

Smart Programming

  • DUP periodization (strength / hypertrophy / power rotation)
  • Auto-deload when recovery drops
  • Wave loading with 3-5% weekly progression
  • Training splits: Full Body, Upper/Lower, PPL, Grappler Hybrid

Live Workout Tracking

  • Pre-workout readiness check-in (sleep, stress, soreness)
  • Per-set weight, reps, RPE logging
  • Rest timer with next-exercise preview
  • Post-workout summary with AI feedback

Recovery Intelligence

  • Whoop auto-sync (HRV, sleep, strain, recovery)
  • Composite readiness score (0-100)
  • Injury tracking with return-to-training protocols
  • Illness logging with symptom-based training advice

Gamification

  • 56 unlockable badges (strength, consistency, volume, combat)
  • XP system with 50 levels (Novice to Legendary Grappler)
  • Weekly challenges with streak shields
  • Shareable PR cards for the group chat
04
Built for Fighters

Not another generic fitness app.

  • Grappling/striking session tracking — BJJ, Muay Thai, MMA, wrestling, judo all supported with intensity + submission stats
  • Dual-training day awareness — lifted and sparred same day? The app knows and adjusts tomorrow
  • Competition prep module — peaking blocks, tapering, weight cut planning for fight week
  • Grip strength tracking — dead hang times, dynamometer scores, directly relevant to grappling
  • Posterior chain emphasis — deadlifts, rows, pull-ups prioritized for grappling performance
  • 172 exercises with grappling-specific flags and form videos

Supported Combat Sports

BJJ Gi No-Gi MMA Muay Thai Boxing Wrestling Judo Kickboxing Sambo Karate

Whoop auto-imports combat sessions by sport type. Every sparring round is factored into your lifting recovery.

Member Journey

From signup to paying subscriber.

Day 1 — Onboard in 2 minutes

Member enters age, experience, equipment, training days, and goals. App generates their first mesocycle instantly.

Week 1 — Hook with free features

Workout logging, streaks, XP, and badges create daily engagement. Members share PRs in the gym group chat.

Week 2 — 14-day Pro trial triggers

After 3 completed workouts, the free Pro trial unlocks. Members experience AI coaching, injury tracking, and nutrition guidance.

Week 3+ — Conversion

Trial ends, soft paywall appears. By now members rely on their training data, streaks, and weekly coach summaries. Conversion feels natural, not forced.
